Prices for the 42mm variant

Prices for the 42mm variant start from €209, and the 46mm version can be found for as low as €229, but the cost grows when a more exclusive color is selected. Huawei will discount some products if purchased in a bundle with the Watch GT 3, but all the promos are market-dependent. However, even without the extra goodies, the GT 3 is one of, if not the best value-for-money smartwatches in 2021. If you need a companion for your runs, that also wouldn't look out of place in fancier environments, you should certainly have this one on your shortlist.
