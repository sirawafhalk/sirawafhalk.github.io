You can swipe up on opened apps to close them by pressing the crown button twice in quick succession on the watch.Step goals should be adjusted dailyYou do have the option to adjust your step goal even if you have been smashing your step counts or if you need to stick to a more reasonable goal until you are comfortable increasing your goals again. Here’s how you can do it the fastest:Tap the Me tab in the Samsung Health app (next to Devices).Then select Goals from SettingsYou can adjust your step goal by dragging left or right. By doing this, you’ll be able to estimate how long it will take you to meet your step goal and how many calories you’ll burn.
